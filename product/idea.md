# Rangde

- Rangde is a platform that builds stories from your moments.
- Moments can be captured in various ways: Photos, Videos, Diary, Voice Notes etc..,.
- Rangde stiches all the moments into stories.

## First Idea:
- An android app that can creates stories from pictures.
- Here story is a collage / template that can fill photos automatically captured on that day.

### User Stories:
- Installs the App from playstore.
- Opens the App, app asks for permissions.
- Post giving permissions, app will scan the photos and stores the metadata in a database.
- End of the day, app will select photos and creates a collage.
- End of the day a notification pops up intimating a story is ready.
- Taps on notification app opens up and a collage is seen.
- Download / Share the collage.
